| Do’s | Don’ts |
| --- | --- |
| Think before posting anything online. | Do not share personal information like address, phone number, or passwords. |
| Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings. | Do not post offensive, abusive, or disrespectful content. |
| Verify information before sharing it. | Do not spread fake news, rumors, or unverified posts. |
| Maintain a positive and professional online image. | Do not damage your reputation by posting inappropriate photos or comments. |
| Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely. | Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online fights. |

**Do’s**

*   Think before you post.
*   Keep your accounts secure.
*   Check facts before sharing.
*   Be respectful in communication.
*   Use social media for learning and networking.

**Don’ts**

*   Don’t share sensitive personal details.
*   Don’t post harmful or rude comments.
*   Don’t believe or spread fake information.
*   Don’t upload inappropriate content.
*   Don’t waste excessive time on social media.
